About Us

The Entrepreneurship Cell is setup with the primary motive of encouraging entrepreneurship amongst the students of our college. Risk taking is inherent in starting anything new. To be an entrepreneur does not mean starting a new company, but the basic approach is to have an innovative idea, starting from scratch and then building on it. Our mission of developing the change makers of the society is achieved by organizing seminars, competitions, workshops, conferences and whatever else you can think of.
